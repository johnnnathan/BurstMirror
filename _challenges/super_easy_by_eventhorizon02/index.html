📝 Solution

Load up ghidra and open the binary. Inside the main function there is a call to "check_pass()". Follow the call and you will see two interesting things. The password is passed to the program through arguments, and the length of the password needs to be a multiple of three, while also being a prime number. Looking up a list of 3 digit prime numbers and passing a few of them as input to the program will grant us a flag. Have a great day :) github.com/johnnnathan
